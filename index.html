Welcome to My Brain

I spent most of my career as a Graphic Designer. But I've always had an interest in programming. The two paths often cross and being fluent in both seemed like a reasonable goal.

Graphic Designer

With a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Graphic Design from Northern Kentucky University, I spent the last 25 plus years designing eveything from printed collateral to displays to signage to websites.

Developer

I've taken my knowledge of layout and design and taught myself over the years how to code websites and other digital media. It started with designing emails and progressed into learning Wordpress and Shopify platforms for website design. Now I'm applying that knowledge to code websites from the ground up.
